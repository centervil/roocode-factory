# Requirements for Issue #24: Standard Procedure for Issue Detailing and Splitting

## Background
Without a standardized approach to issue granularity, individual issues can become over-scoped, making it difficult to maintain the "encapsulation" and "traceability" principles of Issue-Driven Development (IDD).

## Objectives
- Establish a clear standard for issue granularity that can be adapted to any project.
- Embed the concept of "Issue Analysis and Splitting" into the core behavior of the `skill-issue-manager`.

## Scope
- Update `README.md` with a "Issue Granularity Standards" section.
- Update `skill-issue-manager/SKILL.md` to reference these standards during the `Start` phase.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] `README.md` explicitly defines the criteria for splitting issues (e.g., cross-layer work, long duration).
- [ ] `skill-issue-manager` instructions include a step to evaluate issue granularity at the beginning of the development cycle.
